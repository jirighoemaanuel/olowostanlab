Welcome to my page!

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Florida State University. My research focuses primarily on the areas of urban hydrology, hydrologic/hydraulic modeling, sustainable and resilient urban water systems, stormwater management, smart city technologies, surface-groundwater interaction, and impact of nonstationary stressors—climate and land use change on hydrology and biogeochemistry. ​
